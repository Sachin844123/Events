About TechFest

TechFest 2025 is the premier technology event organized by the IT Department of our college. It brings together the brightest minds to compete, learn, and showcase their technical skills across various domains. With five exciting events, there's something for everyone!

Our mission is to foster innovation, creativity, and technical excellence among students. Whether you're a coding expert, design enthusiast, cybersecurity specialist, AI researcher, or gaming pro, TechFest offers you a platform to shine!

Cash Prizes

Win exciting cash prizes totaling over $5,000 across all events!

Certificates

All participants receive certificates. Winners get special recognition!

Industry Judges

Get evaluated by industry experts and receive valuable feedback!

Networking

Connect with tech enthusiasts, industry professionals, and recruiters!
